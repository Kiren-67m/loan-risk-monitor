## Executive Summary

Portfolio health deteriorated sharply in Month 2, with on-time rate dropping 302 basis points to 95.18% and delinquent loans spiking from 180 to 8,650 accounts. The most critical signal is the emergence of 12,669 deteriorating loans (6.91% of portfolio), representing a dramatic shift from zero deterioration in Month 1—this cohort requires immediate triage to prevent cascade defaults.
