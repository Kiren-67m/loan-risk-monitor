## Executive Summary

Month 4 portfolio shows mixed signals: active loans contracted sharply to 90,423 (−47.0% from Month 3's 170,919), yet on-time payment rate improved to 97.33% from 94.66%, indicating a portfolio reduction strategy or mass remediation effort is underway.
